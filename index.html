About the Architect

Ar. Jasmeet Singh
Bagga Architects, Bareilly

Ar. Jasmeet Singh is a Council of Architecture (COA) Registered Architect based in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh. With a Master's in Building Engineering & Management from the School of Planning and Architecture (SPA), New Delhi, he brings rigorous academic training and 8+ years of hands-on professional experience to every project.

His portfolio spans residential bungalows, villas, hospitals, commercial buildings, and interior spaces — all delivered with a commitment to Vastu compliance, modern aesthetics, and cost efficiency.
