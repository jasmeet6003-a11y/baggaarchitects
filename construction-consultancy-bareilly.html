What We Prevent

Construction Problems We Save Bareilly Clients From

These are real, documented issues that homeowners across Bareilly face when building without professional supervision. Bagga Architects prevents all of these through systematic construction consultancy.

⚠️

Concrete Grade Substitution

Contractors use M15 where M20 or M25 is specified. On-site concrete cube testing and slump checks by our consultant verify the actual grade being used. This is the most common structural compromise in Bareilly residential construction.

⚠️

Reduced Steel Diameter

8mm bars installed where 10mm or 12mm is required. Missing stirrups in columns. Insufficient lap lengths. Our site checks at every reinforcement stage ensure structural steel matches drawing specifications before concrete is poured.

⚠️

Overbilling for Quantities

Contractors bill for more material or work than actually executed. Our BOQ-based payment verification ensures every payment corresponds to verified completed work. A major financial protection for homeowners in Bareilly.

⚠️

Drawing Deviations

Room dimensions changed, columns shifted, openings moved — all without the client's knowledge. Once walls are built, corrections cost 3–5× the original work. Our regular site visits catch deviations before they are set in concrete.

⚠️

Poor Waterproofing

Waterproofing skipped or improperly applied on terrace, wet areas, and bathrooms. Leakage problems manifest 1–3 years later and cost far more to repair than to do correctly during construction. We verify waterproofing at every stage.

⚠️

Inadequate Foundation Depth

Foundations not taken to the required depth or width. Soil conditions in Bareilly vary — alluvial areas near canal zones require deeper foundations. Our site visit at foundation stage verifies depth, soil type, and reinforcement before filling begins.
