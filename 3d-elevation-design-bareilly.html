Why 3D Elevation Matters

See Your Home Before You Build It

Building a home is one of the most important investments of your life. Yet most homeowners in Bareilly spend lakhs of rupees on construction without ever seeing what their house will truly look like until the walls are up — and by then, changes are expensive and time-consuming.

A professional 3D elevation design changes everything. Ar. Jasmeet Singh at Bagga Architects uses advanced software to produce photorealistic renders of your home — showing real materials, accurate colours, stone cladding, windows, grilles, landscaping, and lighting — exactly as they will appear on your completed building.

You can request colour changes, swap material finishes, adjust the façade design, or try multiple elevation options — all before construction begins. This saves time, money, and avoids disputes with contractors. Every 3D design by Bagga Architects comes with revision rounds so you are 100% happy before work starts.

Popular Elevation Styles

House Elevation Styles Available in Bareilly

Every homeowner has a different vision. Bagga Architects has designed 3D elevations in all major architectural styles for clients across Bareilly, Pilibhit, Rampur, Shahjahanpur, and Badaun.

Modern / Contemporary

Clean lines, flat roofs, large windows, exposed concrete and glass panels. Minimal ornamentation, striking visual impact. Most popular among Bareilly homeowners building in 2024–25.

Traditional / Classic Indian

Jharokas, arches, carved stone details, warm sandstone and terracotta tones. A timeless look that suits families who want Vastu-aligned traditional architecture with a contemporary finish.

Colonial / Haveli Style

Colonnaded verandahs, high ceilings, symmetrical façades, and ornate detailing. A grand style ideal for large plots and multigenerational homes across Bareilly division.

Fusion / Eclectic

The most requested style — blending modern proportions with traditional ornamental details. Stone cladding, brick texture, and warm lighting combine for a look that's unique to your family.

Duplex & Villa Elevation

G+1 and G+2 duplex homes require a carefully balanced vertical composition. Our 3D renders show each floor as a coherent whole, with rooftop terrace design where applicable.

Commercial Elevation Design

Shops, showrooms, offices, hotels, and hospitals. Commercial elevations prioritise brand visibility, signage placement, customer entry flow, and façade durability for Bareilly's climate.
